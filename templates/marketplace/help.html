Help & support

Answers to common questions. Still stuck? We're a quick email away.

For clients

How do I book a creative?

Search by style, suburb and date, open a profile, then send an enquiry. The creative replies with a quote you can accept, sign and pay — all in your portal.

Is my payment safe?

Deposits and balances are handled by our secure payment provider. You only pay once you've accepted a quote and signed the contract.

How do I get my photos?

When your creative delivers, the gallery appears on your booking page — view, download and favourite your shots there.

What if something goes wrong?

Open your booking and raise a dispute — our team will help mediate. You can also message your creative directly at any time.

For creatives

How do I list my work?

Create an account and follow onboarding — add your portfolio, packages, service area and availability in under 20 minutes.

What does it cost?

Listing is free. Paid plans unlock the full CRM, contracts, invoicing and AI drafting — see Pricing. We never charge per-lead fees.

How do I get paid?

Send a quote, collect the deposit on acceptance, then the balance on delivery — all tracked in your dashboard.

How does verification work?

We check ABN, insurance and (where relevant) a working-with-children check before your profile is marked verified.
