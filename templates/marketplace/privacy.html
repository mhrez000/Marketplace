Privacy Policy

Effective {{ effective }}

{{ BRAND_NAME }} ("we", "us", "our") helps people in Australia find and book photographers and videographers. This policy explains how we collect, use and protect your personal information, in line with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

1. Information we collect

  • Account details — name, email, password and account type (client or creative).
  • Profile & listing data — for creatives: business name, portfolio, packages, service area, ABN and verification documents.
  • Booking data — enquiries, quotes, contracts, messages, event dates and locations.
  • Payment data — processed by our payment provider; we store transaction records, not full card numbers.
  • Usage data — device, browser, IP address and pages viewed, to keep the service secure and improve it.

2. How we use it

To create and run your account, match clients with creatives, process bookings and payments, send service notifications (and, only with your consent, occasional product updates), prevent fraud and meet our legal obligations.

3. When we share it

When you make an enquiry or booking, the relevant client and creative see the information needed to complete it. We also use trusted service providers (hosting, payments, email/SMS) bound to protect your data. We do not sell your personal information. We may disclose information where required by law.

4. Storage & security

We take reasonable technical and organisational steps to protect your information from misuse, loss and unauthorised access. Some providers may store data outside Australia; where they do, we take steps to ensure comparable protection.

5. Your choices & rights

You can access or correct your information from your account settings, adjust notification preferences at any time, and request deletion of your account. Booking, payment and tax records may be retained where the law requires.

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you signed in and the service working, and limited analytics to understand usage. You can control cookies in your browser.

7. Contact & complaints

Questions or a privacy concern? Email privacy@lens.example. If you're not satisfied with our response, you can contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au.
